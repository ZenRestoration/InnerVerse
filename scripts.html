Language That Lands

Words for tender moments

Use these scripts as starting points. Deliver them with a steady voice, slow breath, and relaxed shoulders. Boundaries land best when your body feels safe.

Scripts
Boundary (calm) Protector

“I won’t discuss this while voices are raised. Let’s pause and revisit tomorrow.”

Why it works: sets a condition (tone + time) without blame; invites repair later.

Ask for Presence Belonging

“I don’t need advice—just 10 minutes of presence. Can you be with me?”

Why it works: tells them exactly what would help; finite time makes the ask lighter.

Repair (if needed) Dignity

“I see where I hurt you. I won’t justify it. Here’s what I’ll do differently next time.”

Why it works: separates responsibility from shame; commits to changed behavior.

Self-Permission Rest

“It’s safe to rest. My worth isn’t measured by output.”

Why it works: resets the achievement = worth loop; restores capacity.

Clear Ask Clarity

“I’m looking for (feedback / brainstorming / reassurance). Which are you available for?”

Timeout (self) Safety

“I care about this, and I’m at capacity. I’m going to take 20 minutes and come back steady.”
