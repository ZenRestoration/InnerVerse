The Mynd Map

The 5 Mynd pillars.

These five pillars are your emotional map. You don’t have to master them all at once — you only need to know which pillar you’re currently in.

Imagine your inner world as a journey:

You start by noticing your State (what your nervous system is doing). Then you zoom out to see your Patterns. From there, you clarify your Needs, create Boundaries to protect those needs, and finally Integrate new ways of living into your daily life.

Click into whichever pillar feels most alive for you today:

Pillar 01
State

Learn to recognize fight, flight, freeze, and fawn in real time so you can respond with care instead of shame.

Pillar 02
Patterns

See your repeating emotional loops, family roles, and self-sabotage cycles — and why they began.

Pillar 03
Needs

Identify your emotional and relational needs, separate them from coping habits, and put words to them.

Go to Needs lesson ↗
Pillar 04
Boundaries

Learn to say “no”, set limits, and protect your capacity without hardening your heart.

Go to Boundaries lesson ↗
Pillar 05
Integration

Turn awareness into practice: daily rituals, relational upgrades, and identity shifts over time.

Go to Integration lesson ↗

Not sure where to begin? Take the Emotional Assessment and start with whichever pillar it points you toward. You can always circle back.
