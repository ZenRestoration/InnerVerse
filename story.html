About Mynd & Experience Points™

Mynd is built on a simple belief: emotional skills are human skills. You were never taught how your nervous system works, why certain people trigger you, or how to set boundaries without guilt — but you can learn.

I’m Brook, and I create clear, compassionate emotional education for people who are ready to feel less overwhelmed and more self-led. Mynd and Experience Points™ are evolving projects — digital spaces where you can understand yourself and practice new ways of being, at your own pace.
