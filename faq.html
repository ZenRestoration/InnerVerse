Frequently asked questions

Short, honest answers so you can relax and use this in real life.

FAQ
Is this therapy?

No. Mynd is loving education and practical support. If you’re in crisis or thinking about self-harm, please seek immediate local help.

Do I need to use it every day?

No. Come when life feels heavy or unclear. Learn a practice, feel your breath come back, take one kind step.

What makes this different?

Psychologically sound and spiritually gentle. We focus on nervous-system safety, compassionate language, and actions you can actually take.

Will this overwhelm me with content?

No. The design is quiet and minimal. Fewer, better tools—so you can actually use them.

Is there tracking or data collection?

No chat, no trackers in this MVP. It’s education-first. If we add features later, we’ll keep them opt-in and transparent.
