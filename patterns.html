Pattern Language

Recognize your patterns

Each family includes a why, a body-state, and a need. Pair these with the Regulation Library.

Families
Fear / Anxiety panic, worry, dread

Why
Your brain overestimates danger to keep you safe. What-if loops = uncertainty/out of control.

Body
Fight/flight: shallow breath, tight chest, scanning.

Need
Safety cues, grounding, smaller doable steps.

Sadness / Grief heavy, low, teary

Why
Metabolizing loss or unmet expectations. Grief is love with nowhere to go.

Body
Low energy, withdrawal, heaviness behind eyes.

Need
Permission, warmth, gentle presence.

Anger / Boundary Injury irritation, rage

Why
A value, limit, or dignity was crossed. The guardian wants repair or distance.

Body
Heat, jaw/shoulder tension, urge to act.

Need
Respect, clarity, a calm boundary.

Shame / Guilt “I’m the problem”

Why
Criticism tries to keep you acceptable. Shame confuses “I did” with “I am”.

Body
Collapse/freeze, avoidance, small voice.

Need
Accurate responsibility, compassion, repair if needed.

Confusion / Overwhelm stuck, foggy

Why
Too many open tabs—competing priorities or fear of the wrong choice.

Body
Racing thoughts + low motivation.

Need
Reduce inputs, choose one thing, time-bound focus.

Loneliness / Abandonment unseen, left out

Why
Attachment wound expects disconnection. Old memories color the present.

Body
Ache in chest/throat, sensitivity to distance.

Need
Warmth, co-regulation, direct bids for contact.

Stress / Burnout fried, no capacity

Why
Output without replenishment. The system needs to downshift and re-resource.

Body
Fatigue, irritability, poor focus.

Need
Rest, basics (sleep, food, water, light), reset expectations.

Heartbreak / Loss broken, bereft

Why
Attachment ruptured or a dream dissolved. You’re mourning a future that won’t happen.

Body
Waves of grief, intrusive recall.

Need
Ritual, meaning-making, gentle pacing.

Jealousy / Comparison envy, behind

Why
Someone mirrors a disowned desire. Pain points toward what you want to claim.

Body
Tension + self-criticism loop.

Need
Own desire, self-compassion, your plan.

Numb / Dissociated blank, can’t feel

Why
Capacity exceeded; the system protected you by disconnecting. Adaptive, not a flaw.

Body
Low tone, fog, low impulse.

Need
Gentle activation: light, warmth, movement, sound.

Hopeless / Despair why try, nothing works

Why
The brain generalized past pain into “permanent.” Novelty returns after one real win.

Body
Collapse, global negative bias.

Need
Tiny proof of agency, co-regulation, future seed.
