Anchors

Short lines that land in the body

Keep these simple. Read them out loud with a slower exhale; let your shoulders drop.

Affirmations

“I am safe enough in this moment.”

“Nothing is wrong with me for feeling this.”

“My energy is sacred. I can protect it with love.”

“Clarity grows as I move.”

“Desire is allowed. My path is mine.”

“I can return to myself at my own pace.”

How to use

  • Pick one line only; repeat for a week.
  • Say it on the out-breath; let your jaw unclench.
  • Pair it with one small action (glass of water, open window, short walk).
